I want to also talk about Engelbart English- 1968- A Research Center for Augmenting Human Intellect. The keyword that stands out in the essay is augmenting meaning to enhance or make better. In terms of this essay, it speaks about how the research center was built to create better working abilities for humans with the computer and believed that it could lead to significant improvements in individual and collective intelligence. The essay in a sense Engelbart’s dream as to what he imagines computers to look like and work, it was a visionary work that anticipated many of the developments in computer technology and human-computer interaction that we take for granted today like the mouse and its course position on the computer screen, this design was made so that humans can easily direct the mouse to aspects you want on the screen as we know today, but back then they still had an X,Y design movement of the mouse. Engelbart must be smiling seeing how technology has evolved, from a touchpad mouse and even more AI such as chat GPT, Bert, Microsoft Bot Framework, and Amazon Lex.
