Reflection

Throughout the process of improving my website's user usability and navigation, I am pleased to acknowledge the significant progress I have made. The implementation of user-friendly features such as dedicated blog pages, clickable links, and scrollable images has undoubtedly enhanced the overall browsing experience for visitors. Users can now effortlessly access and explore specific content, resulting in increased engagement and satisfaction.
However, as I reflect on the current state of my website, I cannot ignore the inconsistency in the structure, particularly concerning the side menus. While the majority of the site maintains a consistent design, two out of the five pages display different side menus. This inconsistency arises from technical difficulties, as attempts to implement the old menu code resulted in errors and complications.
Recognizing this inconsistency, I am aware that further improvement is required to ensure a harmonious and cohesive user experience throughout the entire website. Consistency is crucial in creating a seamless and intuitive browsing journey, and I am committed to finding a solution that aligns with both design aesthetics and functional requirements.
In conclusion, while I am proud of the progress made in terms of user usability and navigation enhancements, I remain cognizant of the room for improvement that still exists. I acknowledge that continued learning and refining of my skills are essential to create a polished and consistent website structure. By embracing this growth mindset, I am confident in my ability to tackle any challenges that come my way, ultimately delivering an exceptional user experience on my website.
