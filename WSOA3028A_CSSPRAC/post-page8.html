Changes That Were Implemented

After receiving valuable feedback regarding the readability of my blog page, I decided to take action and make significant changes to enhance the overall user experience. Recognizing the importance of easy-to-read content, I revamped the layout to address the previous challenge where the text expanded into a long, thin block, making it difficult for readers to navigate. In this blog post, I am excited to share the improvements I have made to ensure a seamless and enjoyable reading experience for my audience.
To address the readability issue, I made the decision to introduce a new feature: a dedicated page for each blog post. Now, when users click on a blog title, they are redirected to a separate page that provides a more spacious and user-friendly environment for reading the content. This layout change allows readers to fully immerse themselves in the text without losing track due to the elongated structure of the previous design.
By implementing this new design, I have made significant strides in improving the readability of my blog. Readers can now focus on the content without any distractions, enhancing their comprehension and engagement. Additionally, the separate blog pages enable better organization and navigation, making it easier for users to find specific articles and reference them in the future.
In the design section of my website, I received feedback highlighting two key issues: oversized images and difficulties in understanding wireframes, along with the tedious nature of scrolling to find relevant headings in my notes. To address these concerns, I implemented a revamped design approach.
Firstly, I made the images scrollable and neatly arranged, ensuring they were visually appealing without overwhelming the user. This allowed for a more seamless browsing experience, as users could now explore the images without any hindrances.
Secondly, I introduced a user-friendly navigation system by incorporating links for different design topics. This new feature allows users to easily select the specific topic they wish to read about, eliminating the need for excessive scrolling and ensuring quick access to relevant information.
By improving the image presentation and implementing a convenient navigation system, I aimed to enhance user understanding and streamline the reading experience. These changes empower users to explore design concepts effortlessly, while ensuring that the content is organized and easily accessible, leading to a more enjoyable and informative journey through the design section of my website.
